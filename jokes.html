Why Bag of Words doesn't work?

Context of this joke: In the Bag of Words (BoW) model, the order of words is ignored. It represents text by counting the frequency of each word in a document, treating the text as an unordered collection of words. This approach does not capture the sequence or context in which words appear, focusing solely on word occurrence.
